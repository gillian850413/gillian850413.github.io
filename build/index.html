Billionaire Data Visualization

IS590DV Final Project

By: Hung Yi Lin, Jingxian Na, Pin Huey Chiang, Siyu Liu

This data visualization provides a preliminary analysis of the billionaire dataset. This analysis focused on wealth distribution, wealth differences, gdp among billionaires and citizens in different regions.

Number of Billionaires in each country

The diagram shows the billionaire distribution of each country in 1996, 2011 and 2014. The blue part (2014) demonstrates the largest proportion of the billionaire distribution. On top, the United States is the country with the largest number of billionaire.

Comparison of average worth in billions of individual billionaire among seven regions

Each bar represents the aggregated net worth of each billionaire within each region in 1996, 2001, and 2014. From the plot, we can see that the average net worth is between 2 billion and 4 billion, and there are not much difference among the regions.
